SEA & SOUL

"The ocean and the human body share striking biological and ecological parallels. Both are systems essential for life, with the ocean covering 70% of the Earth's surface and the human body composed of approximately 70% water. The ocean functions as the planet's lungs, producing over half of the oxygen we breathe, while human lungs mirror this role at the individual level. Ocean currents and the circulatory system are alike in their purpose—transporting vital nutrients and regulating temperature. However, just as pollutants like microplastics and toxins disrupt marine ecosystems, human health is impacted by pollutants in water and air. Protecting the ocean is not merely environmental stewardship; it is a necessity for the survival of human life and the planet's equilibrium. "
